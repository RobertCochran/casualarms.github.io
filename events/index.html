Casual ARMS

Community Events

The Casual ARMS events are for all players to join in! We run ARMS custom party lobbies in the "friend lobby" mode, so you just need to add one of the lobby hosts as a Switch friend to participate. The hosts are shown on the promotional banner for each event, which is always found on the Casual ARMS Discord server as well as on Twitter.

Casual ARMS is currently expanding to other games, as well! Look out for Mario Kart 8 Deluxe events in the schedule.

There are two types of events: Leaderboard events where the player's coins are counted and added to the leaderboards. Warmup events are just for fun and/or to let players get in shape for community tournaments.

Leaderboard Lobby Events

The following three weekly events all count for the Casual ARMS leaderboards! All dates and times are in your local time zone unless otherwise specified.

Tidal Tuesday

9:00 PM EDT to 11:00 PM EDT

The Tidal Tuesday event is a later event aimed at our Pacific Time players! We feature a standard party lobby experience so anyone can get the full casual experience outside of our other events.

Wildcard Wednesday

6:00 PM EDT to 8:00 PM EDT

The Wildcard Wednesday event has a unique theme each week! Each theme has a unique mix of active modes and stages. Settings for each theme can be found in the theme list.

Thumpin' Thursday

2:00 PM EDT to 4:00 PM EDT

The Thumpin' Thursday event is perfect for European players. The event features a rotating stage set, swtiching out every 20 coin lobby!

Saturday Party Lobby

2:00 PM EDT to 4:00 PM EDT

The Saturday Party Lobby event features standard party lobbies with VS fights, minigames and items always on!

Community Warmup Events

Warmup events are scheduled before external community events such as tournaments. Everyone can join the warmup lobby regardless of tournament participation! These do not count for Casual ARMS leaderboards. All dates and times are in your local time zone unless otherwise specified.

Mega League Monday Warmup Lobby

4:45 PM EDT to 5:45 PM EDT

The Mega League Monday Warmup Lobby event prepares ARMS players for Mega League Monday, hosted by Schkrimps!

Champions of the Era Warmup Lobby

4:45 PM EDT to 5:45 PM EDT

The Champions of the Era Warmup Lobby event prepares ARMS players for Champions of the Era, hosted by Era Wolf!
