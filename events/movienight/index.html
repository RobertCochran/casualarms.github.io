Casual ARMS

Movie Night

Casual ARMS movie nights is a fun non-gaming event where we get together and watch an interesting film. Movie nights happen most Thursdays at 4:00 PM EDT and are hosted by community member Reis.

The movie is shown, along with community chat, on the Rabbit website.
