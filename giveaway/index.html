Casual ARMS

Super Mario Maker 2 Giveaway


In celebration of the new release of SUPER MARIO MAKER 2, we will be giving away a physical copy of the game at the end of August to one lucky winner who doesn't already own it! All you need to do to sign up is type !giveaway in #bot-channel in the Discord server. Your odds of winning will be determined by your placement in our monthly ARMS leaderboard, so participate in as many of our Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday and Sunday leaderboard events as you can for your best shot!

We ask that anyone who already owns a copy of the game please refrain from entering this giveaway so that those who don't own a copy will have a better chance to earn one of their own. We will contact the winner on September 1st to arrange for shipping. As Casual ARMS is an international community, members from all around the world are welcome to participate.

Be sure to check out the rest of our website for more information on our tournaments and events, including our entire schedule listed in your local time. You can also show your support on our Patreon. All funds raised go back into the server for leaderboard prizes, tournament prizes, and giveaways like this one
