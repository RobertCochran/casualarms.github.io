Super Smash Bros. Ultimate Giveaway


In celebration of the new release of Super Smash Bros. Ultimate, we will be giving away a physical copy of the game at the end of December to one lucky winner who doesn't already own it! All you need to do to sign up is type !giveaway in #bot-channel in the Discord server. Your odds of winning will be determined by your placement in our monthly ARMS leaderboard, so participate in as many of our Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday and Sunday leaderboard events as you can for your best shot!

We ask that anyone who already owns a copy of the game please refrain from entering this giveaway so that those who don't own a copy will have a better chance to earn one of their own. We will contact the winner on January 1st to arrange for shipping.

For more information on our tournaments and events, including our entire schedule listed in your local time and ways to show your support on our Patreon where all funds raised go back into the server for things just like this, be sure to check out the rest of the website.
